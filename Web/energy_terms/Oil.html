What is Oil Energy?
Generating energy from Oil, from Earth Reservoirs

Oil energy provides many different types of transportation fuel and plastics.

However, oil isn't the best energy source because it is finite in supply.

It also emits more greenhouse gases than alternate energy sources, which is not what we want for our climates.
