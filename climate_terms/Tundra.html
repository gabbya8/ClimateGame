What is TUNDRA?
Like Polar, but Wildflowers in Summer!
×

Tundra climates have low precipitation, and is also very cold.

There is also dry snow and winters are long and cold for about 6 to 10 months!!

Summers have long, but cloudy days. The ground remains permanently frozen.

Yet, in the summer, there are bursts of wildflowers that grow, unlike in Polar regions where there is no sign of plant-life!
