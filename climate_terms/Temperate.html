What is TEMPERATE?
Like Mild Climate, but more rainfall
×

Temperate regions have moderate rainfalls, with sometimes a drought.

There are warm summers and cold winters. Very similar to Mild climate, except there are periods of more rainfall and a small period of spontaneous drought.
