What is Hydro?
Uses Flow of Water to Spin a Turbine
×
Hydro energy is a clean fuel source, so the process of generating electricity does not pollute!!!

It relies on the water cycle, which is driven by the sun, and is a reliable form of energy.

Some additional benefits include flood control and irrigation.
